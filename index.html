UIUC Enrollment Data

Inspired by IS308: Race, Gender, and Information Technology, this website aims to provide clean, customizable data visualizations of University of Illinois: Urbana-Champaign (UIUC) enrollment statistics by race. The navigation buttons above lead to the following subpages:
  1. Granular: Specific to a unique combination of term, school, and student level. 2017 - 2023.
  2. Cumulative: Time-series specific to semester (fall, spring, or summer) and student level. 2005 - 2023.
I encourage you to explore the visualizations and draw your own conclusions (or further lines of inquiry) from the data! Best stated by D'Ignazio and Klein,
Examining power means naming and explaining the forces of oppression that are so baked into our daily lives—and into our datasets, our databases, and our algorithms—that we often don’t even see them. Catherine D'Ignazio and Lauren Klein, Data Feminism
